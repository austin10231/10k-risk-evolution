Quick Start

Follow this path to onboard quickly and ensure users authenticate before entering the app workspace.

Step 1 · Sign In / Sign Up

Use the website Sign in or Sign up button first. Authentication is managed by AWS Cognito.

Step 2 · Upload Filing

Choose manual upload or SEC auto-fetch to ingest 10-K data.

Step 3 · Extract + Save

Run risk extraction and store structured records for reuse.

Step 4 · Compare & Agent

Detect year-over-year changes and generate AI findings.

Step 5 · Dashboard / Stock / News

Monitor cross-company risk, market response, and headline pressure.

Step 6 · Team Review

Share outputs and prioritize deep-dive work with analysts.

Note: Direct app access still requires valid Cognito session. Start from Sign in / Sign up if you are not authenticated.
