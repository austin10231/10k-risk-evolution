Why do Sign in / Sign up open a separate page?

The landing page uses a dedicated auth entry page, and authentication is completed by AWS Cognito Hosted UI.

Can I continue with Google directly?

Yes. Click “Continue with Google” in the auth page. It routes to Cognito OAuth with Google as the identity provider.

Why might I not see the Google account chooser popup?

Browser session state can affect behavior. We force account selection in the auth URL, but Google may still auto-select based on active sessions.

Can I use SEC auto-fetch for all filings?

Not always. Some filings or formats may be unavailable; manual upload remains the fallback path.

How are NEW / REMOVED / MODIFIED risks produced?

Risk items are normalized and aligned across years/companies, then classified into structured change categories.

Where are records and table outputs stored?

Structured results and related artifacts are persisted in your configured AWS-backed storage.

How does News connect to risk analysis?

News cards include pressure scoring and risk-linked labels to help analysts triage relevant headlines faster.

What if I encounter ticker mapping issues?

Use manual ticker override in the app configuration and verify company-ticker mapping before running analyses.
